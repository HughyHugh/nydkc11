Caring: Our Way of Life

What is Key Club?

Key Club is an international student-led organization where members develop service, build character, and leadership.

Our Core Values

Leadership

Key club prepares the next generation of leaders right from high school. Our heiarchy gives members a unique experience, both on the local and international level.

Character

Key Club also teaches members about the virtue of responsibility, both in context of a member, and in the context of holding office. All members are expected to go above and beyond in Key Club.

Caring

At Key Club, members of all origins are considered equal. We want everyone to feel that they are actively contributing back to their community.

Our Pledge

I pledge, on my honor, to uphold the Objects of Key Club International; to build my home, school and community; to serve my nation and God; and combat all forces which tend to undermine these institutions.
